## 1 samuel 6

the ark returned to israel.
- philistines consult the prists and the diviners and asked 'what shall we do with the ark of the lord?'
- 'five gold tumors and five gold rats' send a guilt offering 
- ditch the cows, put gold objects in a cart, send back to him as a guilt offering. 
- if it goes up to its own territory then the lord has brought this great disaster on us. 
- if it doesn't, then we will know that it was not his hand that struck us but by chance (even though they have experienced this they still don't believe it is the lord)

- if the cows leads the cart to beth shemesh, but if it doesn't its not the lord and things that have been happening to the philistines has been chance.
- the people of beth shemesh offered burnt offerings and made sacrifices to the lord. the 5 rulers of philistines saw all this and then returned that same day to ekron
- one each for ashdod, gaza, ashkelon, gath, and ekron
- god struck down some of the inhabitants of beth shemesh putting 70 of them to death because they looked into the ark of the lord
- sent messengers to people of kiriath jearim 

main point: doing blasphemous acts to the ark of the lord brings misfortune and this misfortune cannot be tricked, moved, escaped. the only way is to beg for forgiveness and wish that the lord will forgive you
* reflection: am i worthy of the lord's forgiveness? 
* have i been doing things that i would be proud to tell the lord?
